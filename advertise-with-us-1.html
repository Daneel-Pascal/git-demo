What can I advertise?

We accept advertising for four categories:

  • Training programmes and other commercially-organised events
  • Your UK ICF Sponsored Event
  • Coaching Community Events
  • Products and services of interest to coaches, trainers & facilitators

All programmes and events MUST attract Continuing Coach Education Units (CCEUs) or Approved Coach-Specific Training Hours (ACSTHs). Our office can help you understand what is involved in securing these.
