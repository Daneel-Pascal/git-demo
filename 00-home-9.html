About Us

The ICF is the largest coaching professional association with nearly 34000 professional members with 27000 + credentialed coaches.

Here in the UK we are the largest chapter with over 1700 + members with 1300 + credentialed coaches and we are growing all the time.

READ MORE

Membership

Our mission is to support our members and advance the art, science and practice of coaching. Aligned to and being part of the Global ICF vision, focussing on quality and the gold standard in coaching.

Join us to help support you on your coaching journey.
