ABOUT US

ICF: Supporting and Advancing the Coaching Profession

  • The ICF global organisation was formed in 1995, and has become the leading global coaching organisation, with over 34,000 plus members worldwide.
  • It is dedicated to advancing the coaching profession by setting high ethical standards, providing independent certification and building a worldwide network of professional credentialed coaches.
  • The UK Chapter was the first ICF Chapter to be established by members outside of North America and we remain the largest and most active and influential Chapter.
  • It came to life in early 2000 as a result of conversations held between three ICF members from the UK: Elizabeth Ferguson, Aboodi Shabi and Phillippa Fitzpatrick.
  • The first UK ICF Board was a group of volunteers who passionately believed in the professionalisation of coaching – something we still pride ourselves on today.
  • All our UK ICF Board members are professional coaches and work on the board as volunteers. Please feel free to contact us if we can support you in any way.
